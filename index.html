  1. Game On with The PGM!

    The Mozilla Foundation and The PGM Blog are proud to announce an amazing opportunity aimed at helping rising developers show their potential. “Game-On” is an event for everyone, but particularly for students to help showcase their skills. In the arena, you have complete command and flexibility over the choice of platform and programming language. However, Multi-platform, HTML5 Games and Games targeted for FirefoxOS would be given more preference and will have a higher chance of winning.

    You will have precisely a month to work on your project and until final submission you can show us your project for feedback and suggestions. This will not affect your final evaluation in any way. The prizes include a goodie bag courtesy of Mozilla for the winners and a surprise prize for a lucky winner. Contestants will be helped by us and we will also provide you the necessary resources.

    Deadline: 15th May, 2014
